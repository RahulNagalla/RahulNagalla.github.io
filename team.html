President Profile

Bharathi Peddi

She has Masters degree in Software Systems from BITS, Pilani, India, Bachelors degree from IETE, New Delhi, and Special Diploma from GIOE, Secunderabad. She has been a professional in Electronics industry for 25 years.

She is a Strategic thinker, Leader, and has high work ethic. As a Program management expert, she has successfully delivered many complex featured products to market with quality and within budget. She worked at companies like Clarion Corporation of America and Alpine Electronics in USA, and Midas Communications and Sasken in India. Travelled extensively on business to China, India and Japan.
